# font-strech 字体外观

控制元素文本内容的字体可伸缩的外观。

## 语法

```css
font-strech: [
  normal
  || condensed
  || ultra-condensed
  || extra-condensed
  || semi-condensed
  || semi-expanded
  || expanded
  || extra-expanded
  || ultra-expanded
]

eg:
font-strech: condensed;
```

## 属性值

* normal
* condensed
* ultra-condensed
* extra-condensed
* semi-condensed
* semi-expanded
* expanded
* extra-expanded
* ultra-expanded
