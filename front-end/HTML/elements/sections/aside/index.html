aside 元素标签

与内容页面关联度较低的内容,通常位于文章内容的两侧,

<aside>
  子元素 or 文本内容
</aside>

子元素标签#

带有括号的元素必须满足括号内的条件才可以使用

  1. metadata: <style>、 <link rel=''>(属性rel必须存在)

  2. sections: <aside>、<article>、<footer>、<header>、<nav>、<section>、<h2>、<h3>、<h4>、<h5>、<h6>

  3. scripting: <script>、<noscript>、<canvas>、<template>

  4. tabular: <table>

  5. grouping: <blockquote>、<div>、<dl>、<fieldset>、<figure>、<hr>、<ol>、<p>、<pre>、<ul>

  6. embedded: <audio>、<video>、<img>、<map>、<iframe>、<embed>、<object>、<picture>

  7. edit: <ins>、<del>

  8. interactive: <details>、<dialog>

  9. textLevel: <a>、<abbr>、<address>、<b>、<bdi>、<bdo>、<br>、<cite>、<code>、<data>、<dfn>、<em>、<i>、<kbd>、<mark>、<q>、<ruby>、<s>、<samp>、<small>、<span>、<strong>、<sub>、<sup>、<time>、<u>、<var>、<wbr>

  10. forms: <button>、<datelist>、<form>、<input>、<meter>、<output>、<progress>、<select>、<textarea>、<label>

  11. SVGElement: <svg>

  12. MathMLElement: <math>
